> We attach ourselves to our thoughts, body and identity. We assume these things are us, and that is all we are. We assume that we are nothing beyond this finite and limited experience - that only what we can see is what is real.


> The point of this material, separate individual experience is to put you on the opposite end of the spectrum from the truth of creation. That all is one, and one is all. It's the pieces that make the whole.
